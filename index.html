This piece explores the rise and decline of brutalist architecture in Mombasa, highlighting iconic buildings like the Social Security House, Ambalal Building, and UoN Mombasa Campus. Once symbols of post-independence identity and African modernism, these raw concrete structures reflected a rejection of colonial design.
